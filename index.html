Humans have always dreamed of flight

These are amazing feats of engineering, yet they still fall short of the true, unencumbered flight experience humans desire.

How we can improve:

MORPHING GEOMETRY

By changing shape mid-flight, we optimize aerodynamics for the desired position, mimicking the precision of biological flight.
ULTRA-LIGHTWEIGHT

Our skeletal structures use advanced graphene composites that remain virtually weightless while sustaining extreme air pressure.
ELECTRIC THRUST

High-torque electric turbines provide necessary vertical lift without the bulk of combustion, ensuring a seamless flight profile.
SAFETY NET

An integrated ballistic recovery parachute deploys automatically in milliseconds, providing an absolute failsafe for the pilot.
STABILIZED GLIDE

The active tail design balances the entire suit, providing precise multi-directional movement and efficiency.
INTUITIVE NEURAL-LINK

Translating pilot joint movements into aerofoil control via a high-speed network of distributed bio-sensors.

Putting these together, the FALCON is made.
