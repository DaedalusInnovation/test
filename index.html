Humans have always dreamed of flight

These are amazing feats of engineering, yet they still fall short of the true, unencumbered flight experience humans desire.

How we can improve:

MORPHING GEOMETRY

By changing shape mid-flight, we optimize aerodynamics for the desired position, mimicking the precision of avian relatives.
ULTRA-LIGHTWEIGHT

Our skeletal structures use advanced composites that remain virtually weightless while sustaining extreme air pressure.
ELECTRIC PROPULSION

High-torque electric turbines provide necessary thrust without the bulk of combustion, ensuring a seamless flight experience.
SAFETY INTEGRATION

Integrated ballistic parachute systems deploy automatically in milliseconds, providing an absolute safety net for the pilot.
STABILIZED FLIGHT

The tail design maintains efficiency and controlled flight, balancing the entire suit and providing precise multi-directional movement.
INTUITIVE BIO-LINK

Translating pilot joint movements into precise aerofoil control via a network of distributed bio-sensors.

Putting these together, the FALCON is made.
