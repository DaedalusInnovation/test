THE FALCON

Introduced by DaedalusInnovation: Invent the Future

OUR VISION

We believe that humans can experience true fight and share the skies with our avian relatives. Other methods of human flight have come up short in many apsects of the flight experience. Now we take a leap forward into immersion and control with the FALCON.

1. Wings

Our wing design is centered around a morphing ability. By changing shape mid flight we optimize the FALCON's aerodyanmics for the desired flight position. Click to learn more.

2. Body

The body of the FALCON combines an electric powered motor and safety features while ensuring a comfartable and seemless flight experience. Click to learn more.

3. Tail

Our tail design maintains effcient and controlled flgith for the FALCON. It balances the entire suit and provides precise controls to move in any direction. Click to learn more.
